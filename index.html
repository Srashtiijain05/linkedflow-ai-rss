🔧 System Architecture
LinkedFlow AI is a 4-step automated pipeline:

Step 1 — RSS Fetch: Articles are pulled from selected RSS feeds (TechCrunch, HBR, MIT Tech Review, Marketing Week). Each article provides a title and summary for evaluation.

Step 2 — AI Filter: Each article is scored 0–100 using a multi-factor quality engine. It checks for insightfulness, professional relevance, depth of content, and penalizes promotional/spammy language. Only articles scoring above the threshold (default 70) pass.

Step 3 — Post Generation: Passing articles are converted into LinkedIn-ready posts with: strong hook (first line), clean paragraphs, professional tone, optional CTA, and relevant hashtags. Three style modes — Authority, Educational, Opinion.

Step 4 — Output: Posts are displayed in a LinkedIn preview card with one-click Copy to Clipboard.
📊 Filter Scoring Criteria
✅ Passes Filter (Score ≥ 70)
• Contains research, frameworks, data
• Professional language and tone
• Insightful title + detailed summary
• Relevant to industry professionals
• Original perspective or analysis
🚫 Filtered Out (Score < 70)
• Promotional / sales content
• Discount / offer / click-bait language
• Low-depth, generic advice
• Sponsored or partner content
• Missing substantive summary
